Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

ShopMate ("the App") is operated by Momin Ahmed. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use ShopMate on your Shopify store.

Information We Collect

When a shopper creates a poll, we store the following:

  • The Shopify store domain
  • The product ID and product title
  • The shopper's email address (optional, only if provided for vote notifications)
  • Poll responses (yes or no votes)

What We Do Not Collect

ShopMate never collects any information about the friends a shopper shares their poll link with. Friends who receive and vote on a poll are completely anonymous — no name, email, IP address, or any other identifying information is stored about them.

How We Use Information

Poll data is used solely to display vote results to the shopper who created the poll. If an email address is provided, it may be used to notify the shopper when a friend votes. We do not sell, share, or use this data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Poll data is retained as long as the app is installed on your store. When the app is uninstalled, data deletion requests are processed in accordance with Shopify's GDPR webhook requirements.

Third-Party Services

ShopMate uses the following third-party services:

  • Render (hosting)
  • Neon (database)

Merchant Data

We access your Shopify store's product data solely to display product information within the poll widget. We do not modify, sell, or share your store data.
